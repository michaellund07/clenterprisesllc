About

Experienced representation, built on long-term relationships.

Chris Lund founded CL Enterprises, LLC after more than three decades in sales leadership, business development, and customer relationship management across the manufacturing industry — spanning electronics, industrial products, components, metals, cables, and contract manufacturing.

For nearly ten years, CL Enterprises has operated as an independent manufacturers’ representative, working directly with manufacturers on a commission basis to generate new business, develop customer relationships, identify opportunities, support sourcing, and coordinate projects from first conversation to long-term partnership.

Chris is not a department, a call center, or a rotating account team. He is the single point of contact — the relationship — behind every introduction CL Enterprises makes.
