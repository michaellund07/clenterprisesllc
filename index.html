About

A relationship that grows with your business.

Chris Lund founded CL Enterprises, LLC after a career in manufacturing sales and business development — spanning electronics, industrial products, components, metals, cables, and contract manufacturing.

As an independent manufacturers’ representative, CL Enterprises works directly with manufacturers on a commission basis, generating new business and managing each account from the first conversation through long-term partnership.

There is no account hand-off. The attention a manufacturer receives on the first call is the same attention they receive years into the relationship — every opportunity gets the full, personal focus of CL Enterprises.
